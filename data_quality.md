# Data Quality Frameworks

## Introduction
Data quality is the measure of how fit a data set is to serve its specific purpose in Business Intelligence.

## The Six Dimensions of Data Quality
1. **Accuracy**: Reflects real-world entities.
2. **Completeness**: No missing info.
3. **Consistency**: Matches across systems.
4. **Timeliness**: Available when needed.
5. **Validity**: Follows rules/formats.
6. **Integrity**: Maintained relationships.
